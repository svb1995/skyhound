Skyhound — live aircraft flying overhead

Skyhound is a free web app that shows the planes currently flying near your location on a retro split-flap LED departure board, plus an old-school sweeping radar view with you at the centre. For each aircraft it shows the airline, callsign, origin and destination airports, aircraft type, altitude, ground speed and climb or descent rate.

How it works

Skyhound uses your device's location and queries open ADS-B aircraft transponder networks for flights within a radius you choose, anywhere from 2 to 250 kilometres. It runs entirely in your browser, needs no account or sign-up, and does not track or store personal data.

Features

  • Retro LED board that cycles through every aircraft overhead with a smooth marquee.
  • Radar view centred on you, with range rings, a sweeping beam, altitude colour-coding and persistent flight tags.
  • Live callsign, route, aircraft type, altitude, speed and heading for each flight.
  • Adjustable search range, a soft sound ping when new aircraft enter range, and a UTC or local clock.
  • Works worldwide and remembers your location between visits.
